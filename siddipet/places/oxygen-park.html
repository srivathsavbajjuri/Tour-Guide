Oxygen Park

Oxygen Park in Siddipet is a green oasis known for its lush gardens, walking trails, and recreational facilities. It provides a serene environment for visitors to relax and enjoy nature, making it a popular destination for families and nature enthusiasts.

Location: Siddipet, Telangana, India

Activities: Walking, jogging, picnicking

Transportation: Accessible by road, located near major bus routes.
