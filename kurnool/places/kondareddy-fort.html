Kondareddy Fort

Kondareddy Fort, also known as Konda Reddy Buruju, is a historic fort located in Kurnool. It has significant historical importance and offers insights into the region's past. The fort is a popular tourist attraction and provides a glimpse into the architectural style of the period.

Location: Kurnool, Andhra Pradesh, India

Activities: Sightseeing, photography, exploring historical structures

Transportation: Easily accessible by road, located near major bus routes and the Kurnool railway station.
