Kailasagiri Hills

Kailasagiri is a popular hill station located in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, India. The hilltop park offers panoramic views of the city and the Bay of Bengal. It is a popular tourist destination known for its scenic beauty and serene environment.

Location: Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, India

Attractions: The park features a giant statue of Shiva and Parvati, lush green gardens, viewpoints, a toy train, and ropeway rides.

Activities: Hiking, picnicking, sightseeing, and photography.

Visiting Hours: 6:00 AM - 7:30 PM

Entrance Fee: ₹10 for adults, ₹5 for children

Website: Kailasagiri Official Website
