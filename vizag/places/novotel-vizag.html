Novotel Vizag

Novotel Visakhapatnam Varun Beach is a luxury hotel located on the scenic beachfront of Vizag. It offers stunning views of the Bay of Bengal and world-class amenities, making it a perfect choice for both business and leisure travelers.

Location: Beach Road, Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, India

Facilities: Swimming pool, spa, fitness center, restaurants, event spaces

Activities: Beach walks, swimming, dining, business conferences, weddings

Transportation: Easily accessible by road, located near major bus routes and the Vizag railway station.
