Kambalakonda Wildlife Sanctuary

Kambalakonda Wildlife Sanctuary is a protected forest area located near Visakhapatnam (Vizag), Andhra Pradesh. The sanctuary is home to a variety of flora and fauna, including rare and endangered species. It offers trekking, bird watching, and nature trails for visitors.

Location: Near Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, India

Highlights: Trekking, bird watching, nature trails, and guided tours.

Transportation: Accessible by road from Visakhapatnam. Regular buses and taxis are available from the city.
