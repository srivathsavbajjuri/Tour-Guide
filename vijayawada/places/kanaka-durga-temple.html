Kanaka Durga Temple

The Kanaka Durga Temple is one of the most famous and revered temples in Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh. Located on the Indrakeeladri hill, the temple is dedicated to Goddess Kanaka Durga, a form of Goddess Durga. It is a significant pilgrimage site for devotees, attracting visitors from all over the country.

Location: Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh, India

History: The temple has a rich history and is mentioned in various ancient texts. It is believed that Adi Shankaracharya visited this temple and installed the Sri Chakra.

Attractions: The temple offers a panoramic view of the Krishna River. The idol of Goddess Kanaka Durga is considered very powerful, and the temple architecture is stunning.

Activities: Devotees can participate in daily rituals, attend special poojas during festivals like Dussehra, and enjoy the serene atmosphere.

Visiting Hours: 4:00 AM - 9:00 PM

Entrance Fee: Free
