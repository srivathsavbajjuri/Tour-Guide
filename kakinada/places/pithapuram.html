Pithapuram

Pithapuram is a historic town near Kakinada, known for its ancient temples and rich cultural heritage. It is a significant pilgrimage site and attracts devotees from all over the country. The town is steeped in history and tradition.

Location: Pithapuram, Kakinada, Andhra Pradesh, India

Activities: Pilgrimage, sightseeing, exploring ancient temples

Transportation: Accessible by road, with regular bus services from Kakinada. The nearest railway station is in Pithapuram.
