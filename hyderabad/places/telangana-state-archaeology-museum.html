Telangana State Archaeology Museum

The Telangana State Archaeology Museum, also known as the Hyderabad Museum, is one of the oldest museums in Hyderabad, Telangana. Established in 1930 by the last Nizam of Hyderabad, it houses an extensive collection of artifacts, sculptures, paintings, and antiquities that reflect the rich cultural heritage of the region.

Location: Public Gardens, Nampally, Hyderabad, Telangana, India

Exhibits: Ancient sculptures, coins, manuscripts, bronze artifacts, and Ajanta frescoes.

Transportation: Easily accessible by road and public transport from various parts of Hyderabad. Regular buses, taxis, and metro services are available.
