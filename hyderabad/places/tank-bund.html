Tank Bund

Tank Bund is a popular promenade that connects Hyderabad and Secunderabad. It offers a panoramic view of the Hussain Sagar Lake and is adorned with statues of famous Telugu figures.

Location: Hyderabad, Telangana, India

Ticket Price: Free entry

Transportation: Accessible by road, well-connected by local buses and taxis. Nearest metro station is Lakdikapul.
