Shilparamam

Shilparamam is a crafts village located in Madhapur, Hyderabad. It showcases various arts and crafts from across India. The village was conceived with an idea to create an environment for the preservation of traditional crafts.

Location: Madhapur, Hyderabad, Telangana, India

Ticket Price: INR 60 for adults, INR 20 for children

Transportation: Accessible by road, well-connected by local buses and taxis. Nearest metro station is HITEC City.
