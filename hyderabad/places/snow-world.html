Snow World

Snow World is an indoor snow park in Hyderabad where visitors can enjoy snow-based activities and experience sub-zero temperatures amidst artificial snow.

Location: Lower Tank Bund Road, Hyderabad, Telangana, India

Ticket Price: INR 500 per person (includes entry and rental of snow gear)

Transportation: Accessible by road, well-connected by local buses and taxis.
