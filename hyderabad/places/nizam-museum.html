Nizam Museum

The Nizam Museum, also known as the HEH Nizam's Museum, is located in the Purani Haveli in Hyderabad, India. The museum showcases the gifts and mementos presented to the last Nizam of Hyderabad, Osman Ali Khan, Asaf Jah VII, on his silver jubilee celebrations in 1936.

Location: Purani Haveli, Hyderabad, Telangana, India

Exhibits: Vintage cars, gifts, and artifacts, including a golden throne used for the silver jubilee celebrations, gold and diamond studded daggers, and other personal belongings of the Nizam.

Transportation: Accessible by road from different parts of Hyderabad. Regular buses and taxis are available.
