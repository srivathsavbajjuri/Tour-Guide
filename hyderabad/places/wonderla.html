Wonderla

Wonderla Hyderabad is an amusement park known for its thrilling rides, water slides, and entertainment options for all age groups. It is one of the largest amusement parks in India.

Location: Exit No. 13, Outer Ring Road, Hyderabad, Telangana, India

Ticket Price: INR 1199 for adults, INR 949 for children (weekday rates); INR 1399 for adults, INR 1099 for children (weekend rates)

Transportation: Accessible by road, well-connected by local buses and taxis.
