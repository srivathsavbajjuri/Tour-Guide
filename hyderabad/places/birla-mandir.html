Birla Mandir

Birla Mandir is a Hindu temple, built on a 280 feet high hillock called Naubath Pahad on a 13 acres plot. The temple was constructed by the Birla Foundation, which has also constructed several similar temples across India, all of which are known as Birla Mandir.

Location: Hill Fort Road, Hyderabad, Telangana, India

Ticket Price: Free entry

Transportation: Accessible by road, well-connected by local buses and taxis.
