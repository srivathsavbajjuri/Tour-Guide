Tirumala Temple

Tirumala Temple is a famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, situated on Tirumala Hills near Tirupati.

Location: Tirumala, Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh, India

Ticket Price: Varies for different darshans; check official website

Transportation: Regular buses and taxis available from Tirupati city.
