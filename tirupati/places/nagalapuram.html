Nagalapuram

Nagalapuram is a small village near Tirupati in the Chittoor district of Andhra Pradesh, India. It is known for its scenic beauty, waterfalls, and trekking trails, making it a popular destination for nature enthusiasts and trekkers.

Location: Chittoor District, Andhra Pradesh, India

Activities: Trekking, waterfall visits, camping, and nature walks.

Transportation: Accessible by road from Tirupati. Regular buses and private taxis are available.
