Kondagattu Anjaneya Swamy Temple

Kondagattu Anjaneya Swamy Temple is a famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman. It is located in the Kondagattu village near Karimnagar, Telangana. The temple is known for its spiritual significance and attracts devotees from various parts of the country.

Location: Kondagattu, Karimnagar, Telangana, India

Activities: Religious rituals, prayer, spiritual retreat

Transportation: Accessible by road, located near major bus routes.
