Rajarajeshwara Swamy Temple

The Rajarajeshwara Swamy Temple is a famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. It is located in Vemulawada, in the Karimnagar district of Telangana, India. The temple is known for its rich history, beautiful architecture, and spiritual significance.

Location: Vemulawada, Karimnagar, Telangana, India

Activities: Spiritual visits, rituals, festivals, and temple tours

Transportation: Accessible by road from Karimnagar. Regular buses and taxis are available.
